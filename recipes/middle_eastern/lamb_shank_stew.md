# Lamb Shank Stew

#Treat_Day #Full_Meal #Vegetables_Protein

## Cooking Time

- Active Cooking Time: 20 minutes
- Total Cooking Time: 2 hours 20 minutes

## Efficiency Tools

- Slow cooker

## Ingredients

- Lamb shanks [2 units]
- Onion [1 unit]
- Large tomatoes [2 units]
- Red wine [200 milliliters]
- Carrot [1 unit]
- Potatoes [2 units]
- Greek yogurt [1 tablespoon]
- Tomato paste [1 small can]
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cooking oil
- Paprika
- Parsley

## Instructions

1. Pat the lamb shanks dry and season with salt and pepper.
2. In a cast iron pot, add a small amount of cooking oil and sear the lamb shanks over medium-low heat until golden brown on both sides.
3. Add the chopped onion and saut√© until lightly caramelized.
4. Stir in the tomato paste, chopped tomatoes, and a generous amount of paprika.
5. Pour in the red wine and enough water to cover the ingredients.
6. Bring to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and cook for 1 hour.
7. Add the carrots and potatoes, and continue to simmer for another hour, or until the lamb is tender.
8. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with a spoonful of Greek yogurt and fresh parsley before serving.
9. [Optional] Serve with bread.

## Fast Hack

1. Use a slow cooker instead of a cast iron pot.
