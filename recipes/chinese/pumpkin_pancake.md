# Pumpkin Pancake

#Vegetables

## Cooking Time

- Active Cooking Time: 20 minutes
- Total Cooking Time: 40 minutes
- Preparation Time: 30 minutes

## Ingredients

- Peeled and seeded sweet pumpkin [600 grams]
- Glutinous rice flour (or flour for non-sticky version) [360 grams]
- White sugar [50 grams]

## Instructions

1. Peel the pumpkin, remove the seeds, and cut it into large pieces.
2. Steam the pumpkin or cook it in the microwave until soft.
3. While the pumpkin is hot, mash it into a puree and add the white sugar, stirring until it dissolves.
4. Let the pumpkin puree cool down until it is not too hot to handle.
5. Add the glutinous rice flour to the cooled pumpkin puree and knead it into a dough.
6. Divide the dough into small portions and shape them into patties.
7. Heat a little oil in a pan over low heat and pan-fry the pumpkin pancakes until they are golden brown and cooked through.
