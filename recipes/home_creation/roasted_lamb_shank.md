# Roasted Lamb Shank

#Treat_Day #Protein

## Cooking Time

- Active Cooking Time: 20 minutes
- Total Cooking Time: 2 hours 40 minutes
- Preparation Time: 12 hours

## Ingredients

- Lamb shanks [3 units]
- Cumin powder [10 grams]
- Sugar [15 grams]
- Honey [20 grams]
- Salt [15 grams]
- White pepper [3 grams]
- Chili powder [18 grams]
- Soy sauce [20 grams]
- Cooking wine [20 grams]
- Onion [1 unit]

## Instructions

1. Score the lamb shanks down to the bone.
2. In a large bowl, mix together cumin powder, sugar, honey, salt, white pepper, chili powder, soy sauce, and cooking wine to create a marinade.
3. Slice the onion.
4. Rub the marinade evenly over the lamb shanks and top with the sliced onion.
5. Marinate overnight in the refrigerator.
6. Wrap each lamb shank individually in aluminum foil, including any excess marinade and onion.
7. Preheat the oven to 325째F (163째C) and bake for 2.5 hours.
8. Unwrap the lamb shanks, increase the oven temperature to 410째F (210째C), and bake for another 10 minutes until the surface is browned.
